Why choose SamITHealth for RCM

Revenue cycle performance is a combination of process, people, and technology. SamITHealth brings proven operational playbooks, experienced RCM professionals, and analytics to uncover lost revenue and accelerate cash flow. We start with a diagnostic that looks at workflow handoffs, denial patterns, coding variances, and AR waterfall — then build a prioritized remediation plan designed to deliver measurable outcomes within 90 days.

Our programs are tailored to your size and operational maturity: focused remediation for episodic issues, hybrid support for partial outsourcing, or fully managed services for organizations seeking predictable cash flow and regulatory compliance. We emphasize measurable KPIs such as clean claim rate, days in AR, net collection rate, and denial rate. Monthly reconciliations and executive dashboards ensure transparency and continuous improvement.

Clients typically realize faster collections, lower denial rates and improved cash visibility. Whether you need help with a stubborn denial type, a remediation of coding issues, or a full RCM transformation, SamITHealth delivers a practical plan and a team that executes with discipline.
