Our lab startup playbook

We follow a repeatable, milestone-driven process designed to reduce surprises and accelerate time-to-revenue.

  1. Discovery & Planning: Business case, test menu, projected volumes and regulatory paths.
  2. Licensing & Compliance: CLIA, state/local permits, SOP templates and inspection prep.
  3. Technology & Integration: LIS selection, LOINC/CPT mapping, interfaces with EHRs and billing platforms.
  4. Billing & Revenue Setup: Payer enrollment, pricing strategy, claim workflows and scrubbing rules.
  5. Go-Live & Stabilization: Parallel testing, monitoring, and iterative tuning until steady-state.

Long description to attract clients

Starting a laboratory is an entrepreneurial endeavor that requires clinical rigor, operational discipline and deep knowledge of the payer landscape. Many well-intentioned startups succeed clinically but struggle financially because they underestimate payer complexity, code mapping subtleties, or integration requirements. SamITHealth fills that gap by combining laboratory operations expertise with revenue cycle design — ensuring your lab is both clinically excellent and financially sustainable.

We provide hands-on vendor selection support to ensure your LIS, middleware and instruments will integrate smoothly with your chosen EHR and billing systems. Our billing specialists set up claim rules and modifier logic for complex test bundles, reducing rejections and accelerating collections. Moreover, we prepare your team for audits and inspections with CLIA-ready documentation, SOPs and staff training.

For founders and operators, our service offers a pragmatic path to market: lower startup risk, predictable timelines, and an upfront assessment of revenue potential. We can run a pre-launch financial model that connects projected volumes with reimbursement assumptions so you know when the lab will break even. Engage us for a discovery call and we’ll deliver a concise checklist and a recommended pilot scope tailored to your needs.
