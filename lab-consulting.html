Comprehensive lab consulting services

Laboratories operate at the intersection of clinical quality and complex revenue rules. Our consulting services are built to improve both sides of that equation: operational efficiency, testing quality and financial performance.

What we do (detailed)

  • Accessioning & Specimen Flow: Reduce mislabels, improve TAT and automate accessioning where possible.
  • CPT/LOINC Mapping: Correct code sets and crosswalks to avoid denials and underpayment.
  • LIS Integration: Seamless data exchange between LIS, EHR and billing systems to eliminate manual steps.
  • Billing & Claims Management: Payer-specific rules engines, claim scrubbing and appeals workflows tuned for laboratory payers.
  • Quality & Compliance: CLIA-ready documentation, SOPs, and audit support to reduce regulatory risk.
  • Analytics & Continuous Improvement: Dashboarding, root-cause denial analysis, and periodic performance reviews.

Long description to attract clients

Clinical and reference laboratories are facing unprecedented demands: greater test volumes, evolving payer policies, shrinking margins, and heightened regulatory expectations. Our seasoned consultants work side-by-side with lab leadership to map specimen journeys, identify revenue leakage, and implement high-impact changes — from simple workflow fixes to complex LIS integrations.

Engage us for a focused pilot and we’ll deliver a clear remediation plan with estimated financial upside, KPIs, and a roadmap for scale. We also provide training, documentation, and ongoing optimization support to ensure sustainable outcomes.

Client outcomes & supporting services

  • Faster test-to-bill cycle times
  • Reduced denied claims and fewer appeals
  • CLIA and regulatory audit readiness
  • Improved LIS data fidelity and integrations
  • Dashboards for operational and financial KPIs
