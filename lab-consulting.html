Comprehensive lab consulting services

Laboratories operate at the intersection of clinical quality and complex revenue rules. Our consulting services are built to improve both sides of that equation. We diagnose operational bottlenecks, implement practical fixes, and ensure your billing engine captures the full value of every test performed.

What we do (detailed)

  • Accessioning & Specimen Flow: Reduce mislabels, improve TAT and automate accessioning where possible.
  • CPT/LOINC Mapping: Correct code sets and crosswalks to avoid denials and underpayment.
  • LIS Integration: Seamless data exchange between LIS, EHR and billing systems to eliminate manual steps.
  • Billing & Claims Management: Payer-specific rules engines, claim scrubbing and appeals workflows tuned for laboratory payers.
  • Quality & Compliance: CLIA-ready documentation, SOPs, and audit support to reduce regulatory risk.
  • Analytics & Continuous Improvement: Dashboarding, root-cause denial analysis, and periodic performance reviews.

Long description to attract clients

Clinical and reference laboratories are facing unprecedented demands: greater test volumes, evolving payer policies, shrinking margins, and heightened regulatory scrutiny. Every process inefficiency or coding mistake can lead to lost revenue and delayed patient care. SamITHealth offers a laboratory-focused consulting practice that blends deep domain knowledge (CPT/LOINC, CLIA regulations, LIS architectures) with hands-on operational improvements and revenue cycle discipline.

Our seasoned consultants work side-by-side with lab leadership to map specimen journeys, identify revenue leakage, and implement high-impact changes — from simple accessioning fixes to end-to-end LIS integrations. We prioritize measurable outcomes: increased charge capture, fewer payer denials, improved turnaround times, and demonstrable ROI. For labs with complex payer mixes, our billing specialists tune claim logic to handle test bundling, modifier rules, and payer-specific adjudication criteria.

Engage us for a focused pilot and we’ll deliver a clear remediation plan with estimated financial upside. Beyond technical fixes, we train your teams, document SOPs, and set up dashboards so improvements are sustainable. Whether you operate a hospital lab, reference lab, or point-of-care network, SamITHealth brings practical expertise that turns laboratory operations into a strategic asset rather than a cost center.
