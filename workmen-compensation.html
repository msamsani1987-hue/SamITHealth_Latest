Why choose SamITHealth for workers' compensation RCM?

Workers' compensation and auto claims live at the intersection of clinical care, regulatory rules and specialized payer practices. Mistakes are costly: delayed payments, underpayments and audit exposure can significantly impact cash flow. SamITHealth brings deep operational experience in injury-related billing, fee schedules and payer negotiations to help providers and clinics recover revenue while remaining compliant.

We begin with a rapid claim health-check that measures exposure areas: missed billable events, erroneous modifiers, incorrect fee schedule application, and common denial drivers. From there we deploy a tailored program that may include intake redesign, automated repricing against up-to-date state fee schedules, focused denial workstreams and an appeals engine that bundles cases for efficient submission.

Our teams are trained on jurisdictional rules and payer idiosyncrasies. We provide transparent metrics—claims accuracy, days to adjudication, denial rate and recovered dollars—so leadership can see clear ROI. For high-volume practices we implement batch-processing engines, EDI optimizations and reconciliation dashboards that reduce administrative overhead and free clinical teams to focus on patients.
